What is SAMP MOBILE?

SAMP MOBILE is a mobile adaptation of the popular San Andreas Multiplayer (SAMP) game mode. It allows you to connect to SA-MP servers from your Android phone and play with thousands of other players around the world!

Features:

  • Multiplayer gameplay on mobile
  • Compatible with many SA-MP servers
  • Easy controls & smooth experience
  • Lightweight and fast
